We know Bazel

Software can't eat the world until it builds successfully. When you build software with Bazel, you're running the same code that has been refined and tested for years at Google to build heavy-duty, mission-critical infrastructure, services, and applications. What's more, take a peek at this impressive list of Bazel users in both the commercial and open source space. It's practically impossible that software now built by Bazel isn't somehow touching your life every day—and that list keeps growing.

Bazel's Vision is "Any software developer can efficiently build, test, and package any project, of any size or complexity, with tooling that's easy to adopt and extend." We believe, like Google and many others, Bazel has the potential to fulfill this vision. Today, Bazel's tagline "Build and test software of any size, quickly and reliably" is accurate—and we're helping worldwide with Bazel's adoption and with its development.
