Nix + Bazel

Ready for reproducible builds?

Together with Nix, Bazel is integral to delivering byte-for-byte reproducible, composable software systems integrated from hundreds of sources. Tweag's recent Bazel projects include in silico simulations of human physiology, novel static analysis tools for the autopilot software that powers flying autonomous taxis, designing smart contract languages, and improving the performance of third generation blockchains.

Want to know our secret sauce to achieve predictable and reliable success on those high risk projects? Nix + Bazel.

We are the co-creators of Nix and deeply care about software quality so we focus on lightweight methods to get strong quality guarantees. Software, bugs and all, should be reproducible from source today across all developers in your team and 10 years into the future. Bugs, transient ones and all, should be caught as early as possible by automated analysis, even before the test suite runs. Software components, third party vendor packages and all, should be easy to compose without fear of version conflicts.

We help our clients deploy Nix + Bazel because it considers those things.
